# Mods

Mods are **persistent** changes to the game rules.

Each mod has a name, description, function, cost, and rarity.

Mods may be purchased in the shop.

| Name             | Description                       | Function                            | Cost | Rarity   |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- | ---- | -------- |
| Extra Reroll     | Gain +1 reroll per hand           | Increases max rerolls by 1          | 8    | Common   |
| Bonus Hand       | Play one additional hand          | Increases max hands by 1            | 10   | Uncommon |
| Lucky Sixes      | Sixes score x7 instead of x6      | Changes sixes multiplier to 7       | 7    | Common   |
| Hot Streak       | Roll one extra die                | Increases number of dice by 1       | 12   | Rare     |
| Double Down      | Fours score x8 instead of x4      | Changes fours multiplier to 8       | 7    | Common   |
| Ace in the Hole  | Gain +2 rerolls per hand          | Increases max rerolls by 2          | 15   | Rare     |
| High Roller      | Fives score x7 instead of x5      | Changes fives multiplier to 7       | 8    | Common   |
